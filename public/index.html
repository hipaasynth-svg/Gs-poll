A carving of
a lifetime.

An original language of twenty animals, created by Cody Carlson.

This is an introspective process — one that gives you a real, deep visual of the life you've lived. Take as much time as you need. Remember what has made you the person living today, type it in, and press Create. Then look through what comes back and explore it. The more you put in, the heavier the sentiment you'll receive.

You are among the very first to use this. If you're willing, I'd love for you to share back your image and blueprint — both have a download button — and tell me whether the render felt accurate and what it was like to read. For me it touched something: it made me think hard about trials and successes, family and friends. It was a lens into my own life that let me glimpse places long forgotten. I hope it does the same for you.

Your story is never saved to any disk and never lives on any server. Press Create and it's gone. This was made to give you full freedom of memory — a way to set it down for closure, or for comfort. Thanks for being here.
